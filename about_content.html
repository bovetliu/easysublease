本站制作目的，主要在于方便college station村民发布转租，求租,自驾游,搭车,聚聚等。 转租求租非常简单，就是在地图上对应位置右键，然后选择对应选项: 求租的选择want to rent here，转租，求室友的want to lease here 转租出现的图标是红色的，求租紫色的。点击图标，会出现infowindow,再点击source， 就可以导向到对应的CSSA论坛的帖子。本站直观的标明了这个房源离校车线路有多远，离大学有多远，或者周围生活设设施有多远 还有搭车出行功能，首先点击Ride，在地图上右键点出起终点，再点击calculate route， 输入日期，memo，求车还是提供车，然后create发布。 就可以让本站用户看到你提供什么路段的搭车，或者求搭车。 后续的功能有，在地图上发起活动，比如party,野餐。点击图标可以选择"我要参加"

The purpose of this website is for students, staff of TAMU to post/browse sublease and ride-sharing information, To post sublease or request apartment information, please right-click on map, choose corresponding option from the poped up small window.
For ridesharing, first, please click "Ride" tap. Then right click on map to assign origin and end points. Then click "calculate route" server will fill part of information for you, Then please select departure data and category: providing or needing a ride. Then "create"
Python Beautifulsoup package will be used for info extraction and organizing. Hoping that Solr search engine would be applied at end of year 2015. Client-site scripts have been written using Backbone.js. Server-side scripts have changed to (MongoDB+Node.js) from (php and MySQL). I am attempting to realize one single-page-application(SAP) that is RESTFUL and is capable of handling high concurrency user behaviour at minimum cost, a cost which is completely affordable to one common gradaute student.
